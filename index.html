★★★★☆ 3.8/5

✅ Pros

  • • Very affordable
  • • Wide Jeep fitment
  • • Easy to install

❌ Cons

  • • 600D polyester — fades in sun
  • • Not real MOLLE
  • • No airbag certification
  • • Universal fit — loose on some seats
