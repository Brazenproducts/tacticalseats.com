What Makes a Seat Cover “Tactical”?

The word gets thrown around a lot, but a real tactical seat cover has a few non-negotiable features: heavy-duty material (usually Cordura nylon or ballistic polyester), MOLLE webbing for attaching pouches and gear, reinforced stitching, and a fit designed for vehicles that actually go off-road. If it’s made of thin neoprene with some PALS webbing sewn on as an afterthought, it’s not tactical—it’s marketing.
