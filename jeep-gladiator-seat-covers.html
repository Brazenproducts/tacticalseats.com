Best Jeep Gladiator Seat Covers for 2026

Last updated April 2026 · 8 min read

The Gladiator is the best of both worlds—Wrangler capability with a truck bed. But most Gladiator owners don’t baby their rigs. Between the trail, the job site, and weekend hauling, the seats take a beating. Here’s what’s actually worth buying for the JT.

Important Note: Gladiator vs Wrangler JLU Seats

Here’s something a lot of people get wrong: the Gladiator and the Wrangler JLU share the same front seats but have different rear seats. The Gladiator’s rear bench is shallower because the cab is shorter to accommodate the truck bed. If you’re ordering front covers, JLU covers will fit your Gladiator. For the rear, you need Gladiator-specific covers or you’ll have excess material bunching up.
